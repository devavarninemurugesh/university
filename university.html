Our Story

A Vision That Changed Education

Meridian University was founded by visionary educator Dr. Rajan Pillai with a singular mission: to make world-class education accessible to every deserving student in India. What began as a small liberal arts college in Coimbatore with just 200 students has grown into a sprawling 400-acre campus serving over 25,000 students across 12 schools and faculties.

Today, Meridian is recognised globally for its commitment to interdisciplinary research, social impact, and producing graduates who lead with integrity, innovation, and empathy. Our alumni include Cabinet Ministers, Fortune 500 CEOs, Padma Bhushan awardees, and Olympic athletes.

Admissions 2026

Your Journey
Starts Here

Applications for the 2026–27 academic year are now open. Join 25,000+ students building their future at Meridian.

How to Apply

Simple 4-Step Process

1
Register Online
Create your applicant account at admissions.meridianuniversity.edu.in and fill in your basic personal and academic details.
2
Submit Application
Complete the online application form, upload your academic documents, and submit your statement of purpose and recommendation letters.
3
Entrance Exam / Interview
Appear for the relevant national entrance exam (JEE, NEET, CAT, CLAT) or Meridian's own entrance test and personal interview round.
4
Receive Offer Letter
Shortlisted candidates receive their offer letter within 2 weeks. Accept your seat by paying the confirmation fee to secure your admission.

Key Eligibility Requirements

Minimum 60% in Class 12 (PCM/PCB/Commerce/Arts)
Valid national entrance exam score (JEE / NEET / CAT / CLAT / CUET)
English proficiency (IELTS 6.5+ for international students)
Character certificate from previous institution
Two letters of recommendation from teachers or employers
